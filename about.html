Welcome to Ghosh Kitchen

Your culinary journey begins here. We bring you the finest flavors from around the world, crafted with passion and served with love.

Our Story



Established in 2022, Ghosh Kitchen has been a cornerstone in the Kolkata community. Founded by Soumyaneel Ghosh, our restaurant has been committed to delivering a dining experience that is second to none. We pride ourselves on our authentic recipes, which have been passed down through generations, and our commitment to using the freshest ingredients.

Our Mission

At Ghosh Kitchen, our mission is to provide our guests with an unforgettable dining experience.

We are dedicated to offering exceptional service and high-quality dishes that showcase our culinary expertise.

Whether you're here for a casual meal with family or a special occasion, we strive to make every visit a memorable one.



Quality

Our chefs are dedicated to creating dishes that are not only delicious but also visually appealing. We believe that quality is the key to a memorable dining experience.

Service

Our staff is committed to providing exceptional service to our guests. From the moment you walk in, we strive to make you feel welcome and ensure that your dining experience is nothing short of perfect.

Community

We are proud to be a part of the Kolkata community and are dedicated to giving back. We support local charities and organizations and are committed to making a positive impact in our community.
